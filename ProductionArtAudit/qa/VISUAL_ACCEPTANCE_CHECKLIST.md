- [ ] Palette: semantic colours exact where specified (#E63838 #3878E6 #38BF59
      #FAD12E #9938E6 #F28C26); pastels within the sky family.
- [ ] Consistency inside a batch family (icons/chrome/hero read as one set).
- [ ] No AI artefacts: extra limbs/fingers, melted shapes, incoherent ornament,
      noise textures, accidental letters.
- [ ] Mobile readability: check on a phone-sized preview, both bright and dim.
- [ ] Nothing scary, violent, or off-tone for a family audience.
