# Commercial Art Acceptance Checklist

- [ ] Provenance recorded for every shipped/store asset (generator, date,
      prompts kept) in the licence register (see decisions/OPEN_DECISIONS.md D3).
- [ ] Commercial-use rights established for all art (incl. the user-supplied
      outfit sets — currently UNRECORDED: blocker for public release).
- [ ] Wordmark: originality/trademark review done; no traced commercial font.
- [ ] LiberationSans OFL notice retained in distribution.
- [ ] Store assets meet CURRENT official specs (re-verify at submission —
      specs fetched 2026-07-14: icon 512² PNG+alpha ≤1MB; feature 1024×500 no
      alpha; screenshots 320–3840px, max ≤ 2× min).
- [ ] Screenshots are truthful, real captures; no fabricated gameplay.
- [ ] No third-party game elements, trademarks, or lookalike branding.
- [ ] Age-appropriate imagery throughout (family audience; Families policy
      candidacy documented in P31 worksheets).
- [ ] Icon renders correctly under circle/squircle/rounded-square masks.
- [ ] Feature graphic legible at 30% zoom (Play mobile rendering).
