# 05 — World Progression & Rainbow Tower Landmark Plan

Status: `PROPOSED`. The Rainbow Tower is a recurring **distant landmark** that grows closer/clearer
across the ten worlds — conveyed purely through **environment art** (the `landmark_tower_*` asset
per world, doc 18), never a new gameplay mechanic and never a lore-heavy plot.

## Tower visibility progression (10 stages)

| World | Tower stage | Size | Clarity | Distance | Detail |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| W01 Cloud Meadow | first faint glimmer on the horizon | tiny | hazy | very far | silhouette only |
| W02 Enchanted Forest | glimpsed through the trees | tiny+ | hazy | very far | silhouette |
| W03 Crystal Caves | gleaming down a crystal shaft | small | clearer | far | base shape |
| W04 Sunshine Desert | rising beyond the dunes | small+ | clearer | far | tiers hinted |
| W05 Ocean Sky | on a far sky-island | medium | clear | mid-far | tiers visible |
| W06 Candy Cloud Kingdom | candy-bright on the skyline | medium | clear | mid | colour reads |
| W07 Clockwork Heights | ticking just ahead | medium+ | crisp | mid-near | mechanisms |
| W08 Moonlit Dreamscape | shining under the moon, close | large | crisp | near | windows/detail |
| W09 Stormfire Volcano | looming against the firelit sky | large+ | crisp | near | full structure |
| W10 Rainbow Tower Castle | you have arrived — the tower IS the stage | full | full | here | the setting itself |

## Rules

- The tower must be recognisably the **same structure** in every world (consistent silhouette +
  rainbow motif), only nearer/larger/clearer.
- It is a background element (sorting behind gameplay); it must never be mistaken for a platform or
  hazard, and never obstructs the six gameplay colours.
- No connected cutscene/plot is implied — the growth is ambient "we're getting closer."
- Finale (each world's level 10) may feature the tower more prominently in its set-piece (doc 15),
  still art-only.

## World intro rhythm (per doc 12)

Every world runs the ten-level rhythm intro→finale; the tower-stage art is fixed per world and
appears in that world's background + story card + finale.
